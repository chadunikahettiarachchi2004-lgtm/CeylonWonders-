✦ Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 6–8 weeks ahead, especially for peak season (December–March). Last-minute trips are possible but availability can be limited.

Do you handle visas and flights?

We help with ETA (Electronic Travel Authorisation) guidance and can recommend trusted flight agents, but we specialise in in-country experiences rather than international bookings.

What's your cancellation policy?

Cancellations more than 30 days before departure receive a full refund. Within 30 days, a 20% fee applies. We also offer flexible date changes at no extra cost.

Can you design a honeymoon package?

Absolutely — it's one of our specialities. We've designed dozens of romantic itineraries combining boutique stays, private beach access, and intimate cultural moments.
