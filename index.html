✦ Top Destinations

Places That Stay With You

Sri Lanka packs an extraordinary range of landscapes into a small island — highland fog, coastal calm, and ancient stone all within reach.

UNESCO World Heritage

Sigiriya

The 5th-century sky fortress rising 200m from jungle — Sri Lanka's most iconic landmark.

Hill Country

Ella

Misty peaks, tea estates, and the legendary Nine Arches Bridge.

South Coast

Mirissa

Golden crescent beach, whale watching, and laid-back surf culture.

Cultural Capital

Kandy

Sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic set around a glittering lake.

Wildlife

Yala

Home to the world's highest density of wild leopards.
